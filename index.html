My path into disfluency research began during my General Psychology dissertation at the University of Tabriz, supervised by Dr. Mostafa Zarean and Prof. Jana Javakhishvili. Interviewing psychotrauma survivors, I became interested in intentional disfluencies and the potential meaning behind these interruptions.
